Frequently Asked Questions


How does Ai-Recruitment work?

Our AI-powered system analyzes your resume, cover letter, and behavioral values to match you with the best job opportunities.

 What information do I need to provide?

You'll need to upload your resume, cover letter, and specify the behavioral traits you're looking for in a candidate.

 Is my data secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and security measures to ensure that your data is safe and secure.

 How long does the analysis take?

The analysis typically takes a few minutes, depending on the amount of data being processed.

 Can I analyze multiple candidates at once?

Yes, our system allows you to compare multiple candidates against the job description and behavioral values you are looking for.

 What are the benefits of using Ai-Recruitment?

Ai-Recruitment helps to eliminate bias in the hiring process, provides a thorough analysis of candidates, and saves time and effort in finding the best match for the job.
